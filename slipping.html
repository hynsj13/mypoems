slipping

the season has come, yet again
where i've reached the edge
of my mind
out of breath,
choking beneath
all of the time i've spent
running

i can barely stand
with toes curled over
a jagged cliff edge,
coaxing a collapse
i am alone

when suddenly,
my naked shoulders shiver
at a whisper
cradled by
the feathered breeze

"keep slipping"

throw the whole thing
in the water

layered thick in
rediscovered colors
painted by the doves,
the hues skiptrace
like fire dancers
over the skin
of rippling waves

my skies
endless
over and over
i hold my breath
and lose count
like children do

*sploosh*

in the water
as i descend
into darkness
there's a hum
of love
breathing
exhaling
beneath the soles
of my feet

i remember you
i remember me

back
